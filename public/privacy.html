Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Dreami ("we", "our", or "the app") is a sleep companion app. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Data We Collect

Dreami stores all data locally on your device only. We do not operate servers, collect analytics, or transmit any personal data.

  • Sleep log – start time, end time, duration, quality rating, and notes you enter. Stored in device storage (not encrypted).
  • Alarm settings – wake-up time and days. Stored in the device Keychain / Keystore (encrypted).
  • Language preference – your chosen language. Stored in the device Keychain / Keystore (encrypted).
  • Sound preferences – last selected category and timer. Stored in device storage.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email, or any account information.
  • We do not use analytics, advertising SDKs, or crash-reporting services.
  • We do not access your microphone, camera, contacts, or location.
  • We do not share any data with third parties.

Guided Sessions

The "Tonight's Session" feature links to YouTube videos. Tapping a session opens the YouTube app or website. YouTube's own privacy policy applies to those interactions.

Ambient Sounds

Some sounds stream from the Internet Archive (archive.org) under Creative Commons licences. Your IP address may be visible to archive.org when streaming. Bundled sounds play entirely offline.

Permissions

  • Notifications – used only for alarms and bedtime reminders you configure. Never used for marketing.
  • Background audio – keeps ambient sounds playing when the screen is off.

Children

Dreami does not knowingly collect any data from children under 13. All data stays on-device.

Changes to This Policy

If we change this policy, we will update the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.
